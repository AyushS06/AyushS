About Me

Hi! My name is Ayush Sharma and welcome to my portfolio webiste! A quick introduction of me:


I graduated from Alpharetta High School in 2019 and have experience in multiple fields such as Computer Science, Mathematics, Technology, and Engineering.


I co-founded a college consulting and tutoring in 2019 company called 4 Years with my high school friends, Manoj and Jason. 4 Years is a company that helps provide assistance to international and domestics students to place into elite US universities.


I worked with multinational corporations such as Lockheed Martin and Microsoft. At Lockheed Martin, I developed a quadcopter with a team of 3 of my peers. At Microsoft, I helped teach high school and middle school students about the wonders of Computer Science in the language of Java.


From there, I expanded to local schools in my county, Fulton County, such as Creek View Elementary School, Webb Bridge Middle School, Cambridge High School, Johns Creek High School, and Chattahoochee High School.


In all of those schools, I taught students about my honor society, NTHS, and the power of technology in today’s modern world.


Besides my main passion being Computer programming, some of my other passions include soccer, traveling, and photography.
